## README: CLEO Public Resources Google Drive

Welcome to the CLEO Public Resources Google Drive! This shared space is designed to provide easy access to materials, templates, and information for CLEO chapters, volunteers, and partners. Please take a moment to review this README to understand the folder structure, guidelines, and how to contribute effectively.

### Folder Structure and Contents

Here’s a quick overview of the main folders and their purpose:

General CLEO Information

Learn about CLEO’s mission, vision, and goals.

Find general resources such as FAQs, one-pagers, and approved presentations about CLEO.

Chapter Resources

Chapter Management: Templates and tools for managing your chapter, including meeting agendas, budgets, and recruitment tips.

Chapter Setup Guide: Step-by-step instructions and materials to start a new CLEO chapter.

Training Resources

Volunteer Training Resources: Materials for onboarding and training student volunteers.

Senior Training Resources: Lessons and tutorials designed to teach seniors essential technology skills.

Curriculum and Lesson Plans

Monthly Presentations: Structured presentations for chapters running monthly programs.

Quarterly Presentations: Condensed lessons for chapters following a quarterly schedule.

Marketing and Outreach

Promotional Materials: Flyers, email templates, and social media kits for outreach efforts.

School Advertising Materials: Resources specifically for promoting CLEO to schools.

Media: Photos, videos, and CLEO-approved logos for branding and promotions.

Uploads to Review

Temporary storage for contributions or new materials to be reviewed and approved before inclusion in the public resources.

### How to Use This Drive

For CLEO Members and Volunteers:

Navigate to relevant folders for chapter management, training, and marketing materials.

Use templates and guides to streamline operations and ensure consistency across chapters.

For Public Users:

Access resources such as senior lesson plans, CLEO presentations, and promotional materials to learn more about CLEO or start a chapter.

For Contributors:

Place proposed materials in the Uploads to Review folder. Include a short description of the file and its intended purpose in the file name or a note.

### File Naming and Upload Guidelines

File Naming Convention:

Use clear and consistent names for uploaded files. Example:

Month_Topic_Presentation.pdf

Volunteer_Training_Handbook_v1.0.docx

Version Control:

If you’re updating an existing document, include a version number (e.g., v2.0) to avoid confusion.

File Formats:

Use common formats like PDF, Word, or PowerPoint for compatibility.

Approval Process:

Files in the Uploads to Review folder will be reviewed by CLEO admins before being moved to their appropriate folder.
