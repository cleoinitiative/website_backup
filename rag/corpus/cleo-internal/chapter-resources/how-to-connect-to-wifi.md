## Slide 3

Type “Settings” in search bar & then tap on the app saying “Settings”

Click the “Wifi” button below “Airplane Mode”

## Slide 4

Click on your Wifi network
Put in password (if needed)
Wait to connect
When there is a blue checkmark by the network name you clicked, you are connected
