## CLEO Volunteer Recognition, Speech Outlines, and Decor & Setup Ideas

### Volunteer Recognition Ideas

Recognizing CLEO volunteers is an essential part of the graduation ceremony. Here are some thoughtful and impactful ways to show appreciation:

#### 1. Awards

Create fun categories (e.g., “Most Patient Volunteer,” “Tech Guru Award,” or “Best Troubleshooter”).

Present lighthearted trophies, ribbons, or badges.

#### 4. Social Media Recognition

Feature volunteers on CLEO’s social media platforms with a short post or photo from the event.

Include quotes from seniors about the volunteers’ impact.

#### 5. Group Appreciation Activities

Organize a post-event social for volunteers, such as a small pizza party or picnic.

### Speech Outlines

Here are suggested outlines for key speeches during the graduation ceremony:

#### 1. Chapter Leader Welcome Speech

Opening:

Greet everyone and thank them for attending.

Acknowledge the seniors, volunteers, and any special guests.

Main Points:

Share highlights from the year: major events, milestones, or memorable moments.

Reflect on CLEO’s mission and how the chapter has contributed to bridging the digital divide.

Closing:

Thank seniors for their trust and participation.

Applaud volunteers for their dedication and impact.

Encourage continued involvement with CLEO.

#### 2. Volunteer Speech

Opening:

Introduce yourself and your role in CLEO.

Main Points:

Share a personal story or anecdote about your experience with CLEO.

Highlight what you’ve learned from the seniors and how it’s shaped you.

Reflect on the connections made and the value of intergenerational collaboration.

Closing:

Express gratitude to the seniors for their patience and enthusiasm.

Thank fellow volunteers and chapter leaders for their support.

#### 3. Senior Reflection Speech (Optional)

Opening:

Thank CLEO and the volunteers for their efforts.

Main Points:

Share a personal journey: challenges with technology and how CLEO has helped overcome them.

Highlight favorite moments or significant lessons learned.

Closing:

Express gratitude for the relationships built and the knowledge gained.

Encourage CLEO to continue its mission for future participants.

### Decor and Setup Ideas

Create a celebratory and welcoming environment that reflects the significance of the graduation ceremony.

#### 1. General Theme

Combine CLEO’s branding (blue and white) with celebratory accents like gold or silver.

Use banners with messages like “Congratulations Graduates!”

#### 2. Stage or Presentation Area

Decorate with balloons, garlands, or a backdrop featuring CLEO’s logo.

Include a small podium or table for certificates and awards.

#### 3. Tables and Seating

Use tablecloths and centerpieces that match the theme.

#### 4. Photo Area

Create a photo booth with props like graduation hats, CLEO signs, and themed frames.

Add a backdrop with a congratulatory message or CLEO branding.

#### 5. Optional Extras

Display a slideshow of photos from CLEO events over the year.

Play light background music to set the mood.
