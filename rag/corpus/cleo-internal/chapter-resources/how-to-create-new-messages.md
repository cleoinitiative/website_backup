How to Create New Messages

## Slide 2

Find & Click on Messages app

(you might have to swipe left to the App Library & then look up “messages” in the search bar)

## Slide 3

When your messages opens, It may have 2 looks

Having people pinned

Not having people pinned

Note this DOES NOT interfere with starting a message

## Slide 4

Click on this icon

It is in the upper right hand corner

## Slide 5

Type the name of the person you are trying to text

-

If there is more than 1 person with the same name, click on the correct person

## Slide 6

You are now ready
to send your message
