# Agenda

> Source: CLEO Internal Resources - Chapter Resources/Event Resources/1. Meet and Greet/Agenda.docx

### Agenda

Welcome and Introduction (10 minutes)

Greet attendees as they arrive.

Brief overview of CLEO’s mission and goals.

Introduce event facilitators (students and chapter leaders).

Icebreaker Activity (15 minutes)

Choose a simple, fun activity to help participants feel comfortable. Example ideas:

Matching seniors with students based on hobbies or interests.

Small group chats with pre-prepared discussion prompts.

Technology Goals Discussion (15 minutes)

Seniors share their primary goals for learning technology (e.g., email, social media, video calls).

Volunteers share their enthusiasm for teaching and why they joined CLEO.

Overview of CLEO Programs (10 minutes)

Present a brief overview of CLEO’s upcoming activities:

Regular tech support sessions.

Monthly/quarterly presentations and topics.

Fun events like holiday socials and graduation ceremonies.

Distribute handouts with a calendar of events (if available. Template available in Lecture folder).

Tour (Optional) (10 minutes)

If in-person, tour the venue (e.g., library or senior center). Make sure volunteers and seniors know where to go.

Open Q&A and Closing Remarks (10 minutes)

Address any questions from seniors or students.

Thank everyone for attending

### Additional Notes

Time Buffer: Include 5-10 minutes of flexibility in case discussions run long.

Materials Needed:

Name tags.

Discussion prompts or activity guides.

Event calendar or flyers.
