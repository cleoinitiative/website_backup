# Agenda

> Source: CLEO Internal Resources - Chapter Resources/Event Resources/5. Grad Ceremony/Agenda.docx

### Setup Guide

Seating Arrangement:

Arrange chairs in rows facing the stage or podium for attendees.

Reserve a front row for seniors graduating and CLEO chapter leaders.

Provide extra space for wheelchairs or mobility devices.

Stage or Presentation Area:

Set up a podium or table for speeches, certificates, and awards.

Test microphones and sound equipment for clarity.

If virtual, ensure the video conferencing platform is tested, and slides are ready.

Decorations:

Use CLEO branding alongside celebratory decorations (e.g., balloons, banners with “Congratulations Graduates”).

Incorporate blue and white accents to reflect CLEO’s aesthetic.

Place floral arrangements or lights to enhance the stage.

Materials:

Certificates of completion with participant names.

Volunteer recognition tokens (e.g., thank-you cards, small gifts).

Refreshments (optional).

### Agenda

Setup and Volunteer Preparation (30 minutes before start time)

Volunteers arrive to help with decorations, seating, and equipment setup.

Distribute programs (if applicable).

Welcome and Opening Remarks (10 minutes)

Greet attendees and thank them for joining the celebration.

Share a brief overview of CLEO’s accomplishments and impact over the year.

Speeches (15-20 minutes)

Chapter Leader Welcome Speech: Reflect on the year’s journey and successes.

Volunteer Speech (Optional): A short talk from a student volunteer about their experience and connection with seniors.

Senior Reflection (Optional): Invite one or two seniors to share their stories and how CLEO has impacted their lives.

Certificate Presentation (20-30 minutes)

Call up graduating seniors one by one to receive their certificates.

Include a brief statement of their progress or achievements if time permits.

Take photos with seniors and volunteers for social media or CLEO records.

Volunteer Recognition (10-15 minutes)

Acknowledge student volunteers for their dedication and efforts throughout the year. If you would like to give special recognition to Volunteers, use the provided guide in the folder.

Present small tokens of appreciation (e.g., gift cards, CLEO-branded items).

Closing Remarks and Group Photo (10 minutes)

Thank everyone for attending and supporting CLEO.

Encourage continued involvement in CLEO programs.

Gather attendees for a group photo to commemorate the event.

Refreshments and Mingling (Optional – 20-30 minutes)

Provide light refreshments and allow seniors and students to connect informally.

### Post-Event Follow-Up

Collect any leftover materials (certificates, decorations, etc.).

Share event highlights and photos with CLEO National or on social media (with permission).

Send thank-you emails to attendees and volunteers.
