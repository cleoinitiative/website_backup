# Agenda

> Source: CLEO Internal Resources - Chapter Resources/Event Resources/4. Christmas Social/Agenda.docx

Setup and Volunteer Preparation (30 minutes before start time)

Look at Christmas Social Setup Guide in this folder

Welcome and Opening Remarks (10 minutes)

Greet attendees and thank them for joining the CLEO Christmas Social.

Share a brief message about CLEO’s mission and accomplishments this year.

Highlight the schedule of activities and encourage everyone to participate.

Icebreaker Activity (15 minutes)

Use a holiday-themed icebreaker to get everyone involved, such as:

“Holiday Memories”: Each participant shares a favorite holiday tradition.

Holiday Bingo: Use cards themed around Christmas traditions or tech skills.

Games and Activities (30-40 minutes)

Choose engaging and inclusive activities, such as:

Trivia Quiz: Holiday-themed questions or tech-related trivia.

Decorating Contest: Small groups decorate mini holiday crafts or cookies.

Gift Exchange (Optional): Organize a small gift swap if participants agree in advance.

Refreshments and Mingling (20-30 minutes)

Encourage participants to enjoy snacks and drinks while chatting in small groups.

Volunteers should circulate to ensure everyone feels included.

Closing Remarks and Thank Yous (10 minutes)

Wrap up the event with a heartfelt thank you to participants, volunteers, and any contributors.

Share information about upcoming CLEO events (e.g., tech support sessions or graduation ceremony).

Encourage attendees to stay involved with CLEO in the new year.
