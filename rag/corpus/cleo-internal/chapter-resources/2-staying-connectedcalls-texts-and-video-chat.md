## Page 4

Making Phone 
Calls
• How to Make a Phone Call
• Open the Phone app on your device
• Enter a phone number or select a contact from your list
• Tap the Call button to start the call
• Tip: If you use a tablet, you can make calls via the Facetime 
App or internet apps like Skype or WhatsApp
• Answering a Call
• When someone calls, tap Answer to pick up or Decline to 
ignore the call
• On some devices (like an Apple iphone), you can swipe to 
answer

## Page 5

Additional Call 
Features
•
Exploring Call Options
•
Speakerphone: Tap the speaker icon 
to put the call on loudspeaker
•
Mute: Tap the mute button to turn off 
your microphone temporarily
•
Switch to Facetime: change your call 
to Facetime by clicking the 
“Facetime” icon.
•
End Call: Tap the red button to hang 
up when you’re done
•
Call History
•
The Phone app keeps a log of missed, 
incoming, and outgoing calls
•
You can tap on a recent call to redial 
or add it to your contacts

## Page 6

Sending Text 
Messages
• How to Send a Text Message
• Open the Messages app
• Tap the + or new message icon to start a new message
• Type the contact’s name or number in the "To" field
• Write your message in the text box at the bottom of the screen
• Tap Send
• Responding to a Text Message
• When you receive a message, you’ll see a notification on your 
screen
• Tap on the notification to open the message and reply
• Example 
• Try sending a text to a friend! You can experiment by using fun 
emojis-ask the CLEO Volunteers if you don’t know how!

## Page 7

Group Texting
• How to Send a Group Text
• In the Messages app, add multiple contacts in 
the "To" field to create a group conversation
• Every message you send will be shared with all 
members of the group
• Tip: Group texts are great for coordinating with 
family or sharing updates with a group of 
friends
• Managing Group Conversations
• You can mute notifications for a group if you 
don’t want constant alerts
• Tap the group info to see the options to leave 
or mute the group

## Page 8

Introduction to 
Video Chat / 
Facetime
• What is Video Chat?
• Video chat lets you see and talk to 
people in real-time using your 
device’s camera
• Popular apps include Zoom, 
FaceTime , Google Meet, Skype, 
and WhatsApp
• You can use video chat to connect 
with family, friends, or attend 
virtual meetings or social events

## Page 9

Setting Up Video Chat
• How to Use FaceTime
• Open the FaceTime app or 
tap the FaceTime option in 
the Contacts app
• Select a contact, tap their 
number or email, and start 
a video call
• Tip: You can FaceTime 
between iPhones, iPads, 
and Macs
• How to Make a Video 
Call on WhatsApp
• Open the WhatsApp 
app and select a 
contact
• Tap the video camera 
icon at the top of the 
conversation to start a 
video call
• Tip: Make sure you’re 
connected to Wi-Fi 
when using video calls 
to avoid extra data 
charges

## Page 10

Managing Video Chat 
Settings
• Adjusting Video and Audio in Video Calls
• Camera: Tap the camera icon to turn the video 
on or off
• Mute: Tap the microphone icon to mute your 
audio during the call
• End Call: Tap the red button when you’re ready 
to end the video chat
• Gallery View: Switch between seeing one 
person or everyone on the call
• Tip: If your internet connection is slow, try 
turning off your video to improve call quality

## Page 11

Troubleshooting 
Common 
Communication Issues
•
Phone and Text Problems
•
If your calls aren’t going through, check your 
signal strength
•
Restart your phone if it’s acting slow or not 
receiving texts
•
Video Chat Problems
•
If your video freezes or the sound cuts out, try 
moving closer to the Wi-Fi router
•
Turn off your camera if your video call 
connection is poor—it can help with audio clarity
