## CLEO Mission and Vision

### Mission Statement

CLEO’s mission is to bridge the digital divide by empowering students to teach seniors essential technology skills. Through intergenerational learning, CLEO fosters meaningful connections, promotes digital literacy, and empowers communities by enabling seniors to navigate the modern digital world confidently while offering students valuable leadership and service opportunities.

### Vision Statement

CLEO envisions a world where every senior has the digital skills to stay connected and engaged in society, and every student has the chance to grow as a leader and changemaker. By building a network of chapters across schools and communities, CLEO aims to create a future where technology is a bridge—not a barrier—between generations.

### Core Values

Connection: Building meaningful relationships across generations.

Empowerment: Enabling both seniors and students to grow and thrive.

Innovation: Leveraging technology to create a positive impact.

Inclusion: Ensuring that everyone, regardless of background or age, has access to digital tools and education.

Community: Strengthening local and global communities through collaboration and service.

### Why CLEO Matters

For Seniors: Technology is a gateway to staying connected with loved ones, accessing healthcare, managing finances, and more. CLEO helps seniors overcome digital barriers to improve their quality of life.

For Students: CLEO provides leadership opportunities, fosters empathy, and equips young changemakers with the skills to lead impactful initiatives in their communities.

For Communities: CLEO chapters strengthen bonds between schools, senior centers, and local organizations, fostering a sense of shared purpose and collaboration.

### CLEO’s Goals

Expand CLEO chapters nationwide to reach seniors in need of digital literacy support.

Develop comprehensive and accessible training resources to ensure consistent quality across all chapters.

Empower thousands of high school students to take on leadership roles in their communities.

Create measurable and lasting social impact through intergenerational education and connection.
