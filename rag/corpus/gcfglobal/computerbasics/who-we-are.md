## Lesson 1: Who We Are

For almost 20 years, the GCFGlobal.org program has helped millions around the world learn the essential skills they need to live and work in the 21st century. From Microsoft Office and email, to reading, math, and more—GCFGlobal.org offers more than 300 topics, including more than 6,000 lessons, more than 2,000 videos, and more than 50 interactives and games, completely free.

If you're willing to learn, we're ready to teach.

View one tutorial. Complete an entire self-paced course. We believe there’s freedom in the ability to learn what you want, when you want, regardless of income or circumstances. We offer tutorials on the subjects you need to continue your education—or get it started.

Learn anywhere, anytime.

Our content is available 24 hours a day and serves people from around the world. Join the more than 100 million people from all walks of life who have come to GCFGlobal.org to learn the essential skills they need to live and work in the 21st century. All you need is an Internet connection.

Always here. Always growing.

Online education isn’t new. We’ve been here for almost 20 years, and today we offer more tutorials than ever. When you access our self-paced courses, you know they’re up to date—and that they will be available later when you need to review.
