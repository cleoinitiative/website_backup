WHAT ARE COOKIES?

We use cookies, pixels, and other similar technologies. Cookies are small pieces of text sent by your browser to your
    device. Cookies can be persistent (cookies that remain on your device for a set period of time or until you delete
    them) or session (cookies that are deleted as soon as you close your browser). First party cookies are cookies set
    by the website you are visiting, while third party cookies are placed by domains that are not the website you are
    visiting.
