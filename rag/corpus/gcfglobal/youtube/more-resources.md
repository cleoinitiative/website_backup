## More resources

There's almost no limit to the kinds of projects you can create on YouTube. It all depends on how much time and effort you're willing to spend. With that in mind, there are several resources that can help you create better videos and manage your channel more effectively. You'll find some of our favorites below.

## Free resources from YouTube:

As we've mentioned throughout this tutorial, there are a number of great—and free—resources and tutorials available on YouTube:

- YouTube Help Channel

- YouTube Creator Academy

- Video Production Tutorials (curated by YouTube Creator Academy)

- YouTube Copyright Center

## Paid resources:

If you want to learn even more about YouTube, these paid tutorials offer high-quality information and detailed advice. However, they're primarily directed toward those who use YouTube in a professional setting. Unless you're planning to use YouTube at work, these tutorials may not be ideal for you.
